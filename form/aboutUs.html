STORY

Salvador and Nerissa Montero established Kuya Buddy's Shawarma and Quesadillas in 2001, establishing the business's first branch at the Poblacion in Bocaue, Bulacan. The unwavering commitment to provide clients the best in terms of product quality, service, and cleanliness is the driving force behind the success of their business. In this year 2022, Kuya Buddy's Shawarma & Quesadillas created a delivery website with a tracker, an innovation in food delivery that enables consumers to follow the status of the order from the time it's ordered until they receive it. Moreover, in order to provide their consumers even more value, they added Shawarma & Quesadillas offers and promotions to the website.
